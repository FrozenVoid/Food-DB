What contains alcohol if not listed:
Anything which contains sugars/carbs and able to ferment
will contain alcohol .
1.All fermented food, due sugar decomposition.
Includes yogurt,kefir, sauerkraut, kombucha.
2.Sauces, especially named as "fermented".
3.Fruit juices.
4.Soft drinks, due fermentation.
5.Baked products made with alcohol extracts.
