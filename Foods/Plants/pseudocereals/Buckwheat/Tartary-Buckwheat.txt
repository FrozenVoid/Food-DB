Tartary buckwheat is much richer in flavonoid
content and is grown in much wider climactic range.
However the plant contains toxic hydrocarbon naphthalene which
will kill red blood cells. Health websites often promote Tartary Buckwheat
as 'superfood' or as healthy replacement for gluten-based foods.
